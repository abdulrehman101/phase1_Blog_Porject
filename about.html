Cindy Sherman is an American conceptual artist. She is one of the most influential artists in the modern age of photography. This is easy to understand, especially when she has two images on the list of The 10 Most Expensive Photographs in the World. Her photography explores contemporary identity and the nature of representation. These are drawn from the unlimited supply of images from TV, magazines, the internet and art history.
